Product Registration
So that we can provide the best product support and to keep you informed of new developments, please register each software product that you purchase.

As a TI-registered customer, you are entitled to receive these services:

· Purchase of software updates at the discounted update price

· Timely notification of availability of product upgrades

· Factory repair and/or exchange of products

These services are available only if you register your product.

Some third-party products distributed with TI Software Development Systems include separate registration cards addressed directly to the third party. Please fill out and return these cards directly to the address specified to insure that you receive full support for these products
